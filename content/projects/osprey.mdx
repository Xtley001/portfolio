Built to capture persistent funding rate differentials between spot and perp markets while remaining directionally neutral. The system continuously monitors open interest imbalances and adjusts hedge ratios in real time.

**Core mechanics**: long spot + short perp when funding is positive, inverted when negative. Position sizing respects max drawdown constraints per epoch.
